**Which of the following reagent is helpful to differentiate ethylene dichloride and ethylidene chloride?**

```markdown
Ethylene dichloride (ClCH₂-CH₂Cl) gives ethylene glycol with aqueous KOH. Ethylidene chloride (CH₃-CHCl₂) gives acetaldehyde with aqueous KOH. So aqueous KOH differentiates them.
Correct answer is (c) aqueous KOH.
```
