**Assertion: In mono haloarenes, electrophilic substitution occurs at ortho and para positions. Reason: Halogen atom is a ring deactivator**

```markdown
In mono haloarenes, electrophilic substitution occurs at ortho and para positions (ortho/para directing). Halogen is a ring deactivator (due to -I effect) but ortho/para director (due to +R effect). Both statements are true but reason is not the correct explanation for ortho/para direction. The correct explanation is the resonance effect (+R) of halogen.
Correct answer is (b).
```
