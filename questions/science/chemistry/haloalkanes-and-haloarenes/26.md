**Classify the following compounds in the form of alkyl, allylic, vinyl, benzylic halides: (i) CH₃-CH=CH-Cl, (ii) C₆H₅CH₂I, (iii) CH₃-CH(Cl)-CH₃, (iv) CH₂=CH-CH₂-Cl**

```markdown
(i) CH₃-CH=CH-Cl → Cl is attached to a carbon of the double bond (C=C-Cl) → Vinyl halide
(ii) C₆H₅CH₂I → I is attached to a carbon adjacent to benzene ring (benzylic position) → Benzylic halide
(iii) CH₃-CH(Cl)-CH₃ → Cl is attached to a saturated carbon (alkyl group) → Alkyl halide
(iv) CH₂=CH-CH₂-Cl → Cl is attached to a carbon adjacent to a double bond (C=C-C-Cl) → Allylic halide
Correct answer is (c) Vinyl, Benzylic, Alkyl, Allylic.
```
