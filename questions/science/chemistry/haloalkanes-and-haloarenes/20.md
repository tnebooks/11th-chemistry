**Which one of the following is most reactive towards nucleophilic substitution reaction?**

```markdown
Aryl halides with electron withdrawing groups (like -NO₂) at ortho/para positions are more reactive towards nucleophilic substitution. Option (d) has the most activating/deactivating groups for nucleophilic substitution.
Correct answer is (d).
```
