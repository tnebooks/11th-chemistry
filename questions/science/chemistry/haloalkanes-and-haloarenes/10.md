**Benzene reacts with Cl₂ in the presence of FeCl₃ and in absence of sunlight to form**

```markdown
In presence of FeCl₃ (catalyst), benzene undergoes electrophilic substitution to give chlorobenzene. In sunlight, addition occurs to give BHC.
Correct answer is (a) Chlorobenzene.
```
