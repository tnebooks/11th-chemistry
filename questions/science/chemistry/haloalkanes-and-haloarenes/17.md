**The most easily hydrolysed molecule under SN¹ condition is**

```markdown
SN¹ reaction involves formation of stable carbocation. Benzyl chloride forms benzyl carbocation which is highly stabilized by resonance. So it is most easily hydrolysed under SN¹ conditions.
Correct answer is (d) benzyl chloride.
```
