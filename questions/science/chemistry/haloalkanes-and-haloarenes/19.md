**The major products obtained when chlorobenzene is nitrated with HNO₃ and conc H₂SO₄**

```markdown
Chlorobenzene is ortho/para directing. Nitration gives majorly para product (1-chloro-4-nitrobenzene) and ortho product (1-chloro-2-nitrobenzene). Para is the major product.
Correct answer is (a) 1-chloro-4-nitrobenzene.
```
