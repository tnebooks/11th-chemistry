**Of the following compounds, which has the highest boiling point?**

```markdown
Boiling point of alkyl halides depends on molecular weight and surface area. n-Butyl chloride has the highest molecular weight and largest surface area among the given, so it has the highest boiling point.
Correct answer is (a) n-Butyl chloride.
```
