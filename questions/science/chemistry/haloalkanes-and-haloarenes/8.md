**Which of the following compounds will give racemic mixture on nucleophilic substitution by OH⁻ ion?**

```markdown
A racemic mixture is formed when the substrate is chiral or when a chiral center is formed during the reaction. Compound (iii) is the one that gives a racemic mixture on nucleophilic substitution.
Correct answer is (c) (iii).
```
