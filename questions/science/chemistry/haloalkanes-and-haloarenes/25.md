**Silver propionate when refluxed with Bromine in carbon tetrachloride gives**

```markdown
Silver propionate (CH₃CH₂COOAg) with Br₂/CCl₄ undergoes Hunsdiecker reaction to give bromoethane (CH₃CH₂Br). Decarboxylation occurs and Br replaces the carboxylate group.
Correct answer is (c) bromoethane.
```
