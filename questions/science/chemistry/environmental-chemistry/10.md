**Living in the atmosphere of CO is dangerous because it**

```markdown
CO binds with haemoglobin to form carboxyhaemoglobin, which is about 250 times more stable than oxyhaemoglobin. This reduces the oxygen-carrying capacity of blood, leading to oxygen deficiency in tissues.
Correct answer is (c).
```
