**Release of oxides of nitrogen and hydrocarbons into the atmosphere by motor vehicles is prevented by using**

```markdown
Catalytic converters are fitted in vehicle exhaust systems to convert harmful gases like NOx, CO, and hydrocarbons into less harmful gases (N₂, CO₂, H₂O). They contain platinum, palladium, and rhodium as catalysts.
Correct answer is (d) catalytic converters.
```
