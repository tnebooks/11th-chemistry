**The gaseous envelope around the earth is known as atmosphere. The region lying between altitudes of 11-50 km is ______**

```markdown
The atmosphere is divided into layers based on altitude: Troposphere (0-11 km), Stratosphere (11-50 km), Mesosphere (50-85 km), Thermosphere (85-600 km). The region between 11-50 km is the stratosphere.
Correct answer is (d) Stratosphere.
```
