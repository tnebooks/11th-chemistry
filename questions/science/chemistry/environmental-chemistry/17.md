**Assertion (A): Oxygen plays a key role in the troposphere. Reason (R): Troposphere is not responsible for all biological activities.**

```markdown
Assertion is correct - oxygen is essential for respiration and combustion in the troposphere. Reason is false - the troposphere is responsible for most biological activities (weather, clouds, life). So assertion is correct but reason is false.
Correct answer is (d) iv.
```
