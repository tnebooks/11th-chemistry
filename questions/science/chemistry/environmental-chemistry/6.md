**Photochemical smog formed in congested metropolitan cities mainly consists of**

```markdown
Photochemical smog is formed by the reaction of nitrogen oxides (NOx) and hydrocarbons in the presence of sunlight. It mainly consists of ozone (O₃), PAN (peroxyacetyl nitrate), and nitrogen dioxide (NO₂).
Correct answer is (b) Ozone, PAN and NO₂.
```
