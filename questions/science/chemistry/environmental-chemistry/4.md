**Haemoglobin of the blood forms carboxyhaemoglobin with**

```markdown
Carbon monoxide (CO) binds with haemoglobin to form carboxyhaemoglobin. CO has about 250 times greater affinity for haemoglobin than oxygen, which reduces oxygen transport in the blood.
Correct answer is (c) Carbon monoxide.
```
