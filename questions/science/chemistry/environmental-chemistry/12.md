**Biochemical Oxygen Demand value less than 5 ppm indicates a water sample to be**

```markdown
BOD is a measure of organic pollution in water. BOD less than 5 ppm indicates clean water with high dissolved oxygen. High BOD (>5 ppm) indicates pollution.
Correct answer is (c) rich in dissolved oxygen.
```
