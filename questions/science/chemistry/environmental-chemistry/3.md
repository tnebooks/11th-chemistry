**Bhopal Gas Tragedy is a case of ______**

```markdown
The Bhopal Gas Tragedy (1984) involved the release of methyl isocyanate (MIC) gas from a pesticide plant. This caused massive air pollution and thousands of deaths. It is a case of air pollution.
Correct answer is (b) air pollution.
```
