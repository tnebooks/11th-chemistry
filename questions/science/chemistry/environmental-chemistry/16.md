**Assertion (A): Excessive use of chlorinated pesticide causes soil and water pollution. Reason (R): Such pesticides are non-biodegradable.**

```markdown
Chlorinated pesticides like DDT, aldrin, etc., are non-biodegradable and persist in the environment. They accumulate in soil and water, causing pollution. Both assertion and reason are correct and reason correctly explains the assertion.
Correct answer is (a) i.
```
