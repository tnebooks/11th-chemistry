**What is the density of N₂ gas at 227°C and 5.00 atm pressure? (R = 0.082 L atm K⁻¹ mol⁻¹)**

```markdown
T = 227 + 273 = 500 K.
Density = PM/RT = (5 × 28) / (0.082 × 500) = 140 / 41 = 3.414 g/L ≈ 3.41 g/L.
Correct answer is (c) 3.41 g/L.
```
