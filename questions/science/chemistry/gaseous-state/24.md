**Which of the following diagrams correctly describes the behaviour of a fixed mass of an ideal gas? (T is measured in K)**

```markdown
For a fixed mass of ideal gas, PV = nRT. The correct graph is PV vs T being a straight line. Also, P vs V at constant T is a hyperbola. Among the given options, the correct diagram is the one showing PV ∝ T with positive slope passing through origin. Option (c) correctly represents this.
Correct answer is (c).
```
