```markdown
NH₃ has high polarizability and strong intermolecular hydrogen bonding. It also has high 'a' value (van der Waals constant). Therefore, NH₃ shows maximum deviation from ideal gas behavior among the given gases.
Correct answer is (b) NH₃ (g).
```
