**If temperature and volume of an ideal gas is increased to twice its values, the initial pressure P becomes**

```markdown
PV = nRT. If T becomes 2T and V becomes 2V, then P = nR(2T)/(2V) = nRT/V = P (unchanged).
Correct answer is (c) P.
```
