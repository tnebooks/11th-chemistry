**Equal weights of methane and oxygen are mixed in an empty container at 298 K. The fraction of total pressure exerted by oxygen is**

```markdown
Let mass of each gas = w g.
Moles of CH₄ = w/16, moles of O₂ = w/32.
Total moles = w/16 + w/32 = 3w/32.
Mole fraction of O₂ = (w/32) / (3w/32) = 1/3.
Partial pressure of O₂ = mole fraction × total pressure = (1/3)P.
Correct answer is (a) 1/3.
```
