**The variation of volume V with temperature T, keeping pressure constant is called the coefficient of thermal expansion α = (1/V)(∂V/∂T)ₚ. For an ideal gas α is equal to**

```markdown
For ideal gas: PV = nRT → V = nRT/P.
∂V/∂T = nR/P.
α = (1/V)(nR/P) = (1/(nRT/P))(nR/P) = 1/T.
Correct answer is (b) 1/T.
```
