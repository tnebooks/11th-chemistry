**A bottle of ammonia and a bottle of HCl connected through a long tube are opened simultaneously at both ends. The white ammonium chloride ring first formed will be**

```markdown
NH₃ (molar mass = 17) diffuses faster than HCl (molar mass = 36.5) because rate ∝ 1/√M. NH₃ travels faster and covers more distance. The white ring of NH₄Cl forms closer to the HCl bottle (where HCl comes from), because NH₃ travels farther before meeting HCl.
Correct answer is (b) Near the hydrogen chloride bottle.
```
