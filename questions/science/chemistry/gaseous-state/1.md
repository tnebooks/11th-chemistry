**Gases deviate from ideal behavior at high pressure. Which of the following statement(s) is correct for non-ideality?**

```markdown
At high pressure, gas molecules come closer to each other, making intermolecular forces of attraction significant. This causes deviation from ideal behavior. Collisions being enormous does not explain non-ideality; molecules do not move in one direction; gas volume does not become insignificant.
Correct answer is (d).
```
