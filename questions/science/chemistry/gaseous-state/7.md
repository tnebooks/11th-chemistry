**In a closed room of 1000 m³ a perfume bottle is opened up. The room develops a smell. This is due to which property of gases?**

```markdown
The smell of perfume spreads throughout the room due to diffusion of gas molecules. Gas molecules move randomly and spread from high concentration to low concentration region. This property is called diffusion.
Correct answer is (c) diffusion.
```
