**Four gases P, Q, R and S have almost same values of 'b' but their 'a' values are in the order Q < R < S < P. At a particular temperature, among the four gases the most easily liquefiable one is**

```markdown
The van der Waals constant 'a' is a measure of intermolecular attractive forces. Higher the value of 'a', stronger the intermolecular forces, easier the liquefaction. Since P has the highest 'a' value, P is most easily liquefiable.
Correct answer is (a) P.
```
