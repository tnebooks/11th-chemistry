**25g of each of the following gases are taken at 27°C and 600 mm Hg pressure. Which of these will have the least volume?**

```markdown
At same temperature, pressure and mass, volume is inversely proportional to molar mass (V ∝ n ∝ 1/M). The gas with highest molar mass will have the least volume. Molar masses: HBr = 81, HCl = 36.5, HF = 20, HI = 128. HI has the highest molar mass, so it will have the least volume.
Correct answer is (d) HI.
```
