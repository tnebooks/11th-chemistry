**Compressibility factor for CO₂ at 400 K and 71.0 bar is 0.8697. The molar volume of CO₂ under these conditions is**

```markdown
Compressibility factor Z = PV/nRT. For 1 mole: Z = PV/RT.
V = ZRT/P = 0.8697 × 0.08314 × 400 / 71 = 0.8697 × 33.256 / 71 = 28.92 / 71 = 0.407 dm³ ≈ 0.41 dm³.
Correct answer is (c) 0.41 dm³.
```
