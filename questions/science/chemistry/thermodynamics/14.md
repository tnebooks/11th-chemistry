**Change in internal energy, when 4 kJ of work is done on the system and 1 kJ of heat is given out by the system is**

```markdown
First law: ΔU = q + w
Heat given out by system: q = -1 kJ (negative)
Work done on the system: w = +4 kJ (positive)
ΔU = -1 + 4 = +3 kJ
Correct answer is (c) +3 kJ.
```
