**The intensive property among the quantities below is**

```markdown
An intensive property is independent of the amount of substance. Mass, volume, and enthalpy are extensive properties (depend on amount). Mass/volume = density, which is an intensive property. It does not depend on the amount of substance.
Correct answer is (d) mass/volume.
```
