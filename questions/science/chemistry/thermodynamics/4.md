**In a reversible process, the change in entropy of the universe is**

```markdown
For a reversible process, the total entropy change of the universe (system + surroundings) is zero: ΔS_universe = 0. For an irreversible (spontaneous) process, ΔS_universe > 0. Since the process is reversible, it is at equilibrium.
Correct answer is (d) = 0.
```
