When 15.68 litres of a gas mixture of methane and propane are fully combusted at \( 0^{\circ}\mathrm{C} \) and 1 atmosphere, 32 litres of oxygen at the same temperature and pressure are consumed. The amount of heat released from this combustion in kJ is \( (\Delta H_{\mathrm{C}}(\mathrm{CH}_{4}) = -890 \ \mathrm{kJ \ mol^{-1}} \) and \( \Delta H_{\mathrm{C}}(\mathrm{C}_{3}\mathrm{H}_{8}) = -2220 \ \mathrm{kJ \ mol^{-1}} \)
