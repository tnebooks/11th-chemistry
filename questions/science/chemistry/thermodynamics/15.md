**The work done by the liberated gas when 55.85 g of iron (molar mass 55.85 g mol⁻¹) reacts with hydrochloric acid in an open beaker at 25°C**

```markdown
Fe + 2HCl → FeCl₂ + H₂(g)
55.85 g Fe = 1 mole Fe → 1 mole H₂ gas liberated.
Work done by gas at constant pressure: w = -PΔV = -ΔnRT
Δn = 1 mole (gas produced), R = 8.314 J K⁻¹ mol⁻¹, T = 298 K
w = -1 × 8.314 × 298 = -2477 J = -2.477 kJ ≈ -2.48 kJ
Correct answer is (a) -2.48 kJ.
```
