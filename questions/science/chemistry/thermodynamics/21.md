**The temperature of the system decreases in an ______**

```markdown
In adiabatic expansion, the system does work on the surroundings using its internal energy. Since no heat is exchanged (q = 0), the internal energy decreases, causing a decrease in temperature. In adiabatic compression, temperature increases.
Correct answer is (c) adiabatic expansion.
```
