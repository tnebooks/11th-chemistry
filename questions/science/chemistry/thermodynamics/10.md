choices:
  - "graphite has more energy than diamond"
  - "both are equally stable"
  - "stability cannot be predicted"
answers:
  - "graphite is more stable than diamond"
---

**C(diamond) → C(graphite), ΔH = -ve, this indicates that**

```markdown
ΔH is negative, meaning the reaction is exothermic. Energy is released when diamond converts to graphite. This means graphite has lower energy (more stable) than diamond. Lower energy = more stability.
Correct answer is (a) graphite is more stable than diamond.
```
