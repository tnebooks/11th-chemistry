**In an isothermal reversible compression of an ideal gas, the sign of q, ΔS and w are respectively**

```markdown
In isothermal reversible compression: work is done on the system (w = +ve). Heat is released (q = -ve). Entropy of the system decreases (ΔS = -ve) because volume decreases (less disorder).
So q = -, ΔS = -, w = +.
Correct answer is (d) (-, -, +).
```
