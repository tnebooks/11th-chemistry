All spontaneous processes occur in the direction that leads to a decrease in free energy (ΔG < 0). At constant temperature and pressure, spontaneity is determined by Gibbs free energy. Entropy of the universe increases (ΔS_universe > 0), but free energy decreases.
Correct answer is (d) decrease in free energy.
