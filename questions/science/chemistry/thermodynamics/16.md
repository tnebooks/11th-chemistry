**The value of ΔH for cooling 2 moles of an ideal monatomic gas from 125°C to 25°C at constant pressure will be (given Cₚ = 5/2 R)**

```markdown
ΔH = n Cₚ ΔT = 2 × (5/2 R) × (25 - 125) = 2 × (5/2 R) × (-100) = 5R × (-100) = -500 R
Correct answer is (b) -500 R.
```
