**An ideal gas expands from the volume of 1 × 10⁻³ m³ to 1 × 10⁻² m³ at 300 K against a constant pressure of 1 × 10⁵ Nm⁻². The work done is**

```markdown
Work done against constant external pressure: w = -P_ext(V₂ - V₁)
w = -1 × 10⁵ × (1 × 10⁻² - 1 × 10⁻³) = -1 × 10⁵ × (9 × 10⁻³) = -900 J
Correct answer is (a) -900 J.
```
