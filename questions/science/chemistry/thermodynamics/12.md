**Which of the following is not a thermodynamic function?**

```markdown
Internal energy (U), enthalpy (H), and entropy (S) are all thermodynamic state functions. Frictional energy is not a thermodynamic function; it is a form of energy lost due to friction and depends on the path.
Correct answer is (d) frictional energy.
```
