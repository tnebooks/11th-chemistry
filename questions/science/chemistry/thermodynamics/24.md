**ΔS is expected to be maximum for the reaction**

```markdown
Entropy change (ΔS) is maximum when the number of gaseous moles increases significantly. In CaCO₃(s) → CaO(s) + CO₂(g), one mole of solid produces one mole of gas (Δn_g = 1). This increases disorder significantly. In other reactions, Δn_g is 0 or negative.
Correct answer is (d) CaCO₃(S) → CaO(S) + CO₂(g).
```
