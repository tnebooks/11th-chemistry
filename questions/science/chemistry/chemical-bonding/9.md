**In ClF₃, NF₃ and BF₃ molecules the chlorine, nitrogen and boron atoms are**

```markdown
ClF₃: Cl has 5 electron pairs (3 bond pairs + 2 lone pairs) → sp³d
NF₃: N has 4 electron pairs (3 bond pairs + 1 lone pair) → sp³
BF₃: B has 3 bond pairs, no lone pairs → sp²
Correct answer is (d) sp³d, sp³ and sp² hybridised respectively.
```
