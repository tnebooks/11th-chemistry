**Which of the following is electron deficient?**

```markdown
BH₃ (borane) has only 6 electrons around boron (3 bonds, no lone pairs), making it electron deficient. NH₃ and PH₃ have 8 electrons (complete octet). (CH₃)₂ is a neutral covalent molecule with complete octets.
Correct answer is (c) BH₃.
```
