**Assertion: Oxygen molecule is paramagnetic. Reason: It has two unpaired electrons in its bonding molecular orbital.**

```markdown
O₂ is paramagnetic due to two unpaired electrons. However, these unpaired electrons are in antibonding molecular orbitals (π*2p), not bonding molecular orbitals. The assertion is true but reason is false.
Correct answer is (c).
```
