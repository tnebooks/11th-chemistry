**Bond order of a species is 2.5 and the number of electrons in its bonding molecular orbital is found to be 8. The number of electrons in its antibonding molecular orbital is**

```markdown
Bond order = (Nb - Na)/2
2.5 = (8 - Na)/2 → 5 = 8 - Na → Na = 3
Correct answer is (a) three.
```
