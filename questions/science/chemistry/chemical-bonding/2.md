**In the molecule Oₐ = C = O_B, the formal charge on Oₐ, C and O_B are respectively.**

```markdown
Formal charge = Valence electrons - (Non-bonding electrons + Bonding electrons/2)
For Oₐ: 6 - (4 + 4/2) = 6 - 6 = 0
For C: 4 - (0 + 8/2) = 4 - 4 = 0
For O_B: 6 - (4 + 4/2) = 6 - 6 = 0
Correct answer is (d) 0, 0, 0.
```
