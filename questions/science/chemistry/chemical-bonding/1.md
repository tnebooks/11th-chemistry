**In which of the following compounds does the central atom obey the octet rule?**

```markdown
Octet rule states that atoms tend to have 8 electrons in their valence shell.
XeF₄: Xe has 10 electrons (expanded octet)
AlCl₃: Al has 6 electrons (incomplete octet)
SF₆: S has 12 electrons (expanded octet)
SCl₂: S has 8 electrons (6 from S + 1 from each Cl) → obeys octet rule.
Correct answer is (d) SCl₂.
```
