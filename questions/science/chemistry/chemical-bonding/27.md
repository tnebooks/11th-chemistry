**Non-zero dipole moment is shown by**

```markdown
CO₂: zero dipole moment (linear, symmetrical)
p-dichlorobenzene: zero dipole moment (symmetrical)
CCl₄: zero dipole moment (symmetrical tetrahedral)
Water (H₂O): non-zero dipole moment (bent shape, unsymmetrical)
Correct answer is (d) water.
```
