**When CaC₂ is heated in atmospheric nitrogen in an electric furnace the compound formed is**

```markdown
CaC₂ + N₂ → CaCN₂ (calcium cyanamide) + C. Calcium cyanamide is also represented as CaNCN. This reaction is used for the preparation of calcium cyanamide.
Correct answer is (b) CaNCN.
```
