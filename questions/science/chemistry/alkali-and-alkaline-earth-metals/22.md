**The name 'Blue John' is given to which of the following compounds?**

```markdown
'Blue John' is a mineral form of calcium fluoride (CaF₂), also known as fluorite. It is a violet-blue colored variety of fluorite found in Derbyshire, England.
Correct answer is (b) CaF₂.
```
