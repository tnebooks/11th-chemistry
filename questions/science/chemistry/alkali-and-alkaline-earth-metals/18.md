**The suspension of slaked lime in water is known as (NEET Phase - II)**

```markdown
Slaked lime is Ca(OH)₂. Its suspension in water is called milk of lime (or lime milk). Lime water is the clear solution of Ca(OH)₂. Quick lime is CaO.
Correct answer is (c) milk of lime.
```
