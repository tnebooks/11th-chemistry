**Assertion: BeSO₄ is soluble in water while BaSO₄ is not. Reason: Hydration energy decreases down the group from Be to Ba and lattice energy remains almost constant.**

```markdown
BeSO₄ is soluble because Be²⁺ has high hydration energy due to small size, which overcomes the lattice energy. BaSO₄ is insoluble because Ba²⁺ has low hydration energy while lattice energy remains high. Both assertion and reason are true, and the reason correctly explains the assertion.
Correct answer is (a).
```
