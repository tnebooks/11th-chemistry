**In which process, fused sodium hydroxide is electrolysed for extraction of sodium?**

```markdown
Sodium is extracted by electrolysis of fused sodium hydroxide (NaOH) in Castner's process. Down's process uses fused NaCl. Cyanide process is used for gold and silver extraction.
Correct answer is (a) Castner's process.
```
