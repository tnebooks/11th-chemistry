**In context with beryllium, which one of the following statements is incorrect? (NEET Phase - 2)**

```markdown
Beryllium salts are extensively hydrolysed due to high charge density of Be²⁺, not rarely hydrolysed. Other statements are correct: Be is rendered passive by HNO₃ (due to oxide layer), Be forms Be₂C (beryllium carbide), and BeH₂ is electron deficient and polymeric.
Correct answer is (c).
```
