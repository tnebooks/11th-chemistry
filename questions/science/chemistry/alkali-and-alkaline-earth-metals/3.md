**Which of the following compounds will not evolve H₂ gas on reaction with alkali metals?**

```markdown
Alkali metals react with compounds having acidic hydrogen (O-H bond) to release H₂ gas. Ethanoic acid (COOH), ethanol (OH), and phenol (OH) all have acidic hydrogen and release H₂ with alkali metals. Therefore, all three evolve H₂ gas.
Correct answer is (d) none of these.
```
