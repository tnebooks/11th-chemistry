**RbO₂ is**

```markdown
RbO₂ is rubidium superoxide (Rb⁺O₂⁻). The superoxide ion (O₂⁻) has one unpaired electron, so it is paramagnetic. RbO₂ is a superoxide, not a peroxide (which would be Rb₂O₂). Therefore, RbO₂ is superoxide and paramagnetic.
Correct answer is (a) superoxide and paramagnetic.
```
