**In case of alkali metal halides, the ionic character increases in the order**

```markdown
Ionic character of alkali metal halides depends on the electronegativity difference between the metal and halogen. The order of electronegativity of halogens: F > Cl > Br > I. The ionic character increases with increasing electronegativity difference: MI < MBr < MCl < MF.
Correct answer is (b) MI < MBr < MCl < MF.
```
