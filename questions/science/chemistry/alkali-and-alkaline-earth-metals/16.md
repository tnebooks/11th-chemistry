**Which is the correct sequence of solubility of carbonates of alkaline earth metals?**

```markdown
Solubility of alkaline earth metal carbonates decreases down the group because lattice energy remains almost constant while hydration energy decreases. The correct order of solubility: MgCO₃ > CaCO₃ > SrCO₃ > BaCO₃.
Correct answer is (b).
```
