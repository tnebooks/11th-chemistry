**Which of the following carbocation will be most stable?**

```markdown
Stability of carbocations: Ph₃C⁺ (triphenylmethyl cation) is highly stabilized by resonance of three phenyl rings. It is the most stable carbocation.
Order: Ph₃C⁺ > CH₂=CH-CH₂⁺ > (CH₃)₂⁺CH > CH₃⁺-CH₂⁻.
Correct answer is (a) Ph₃C⁺.
```
