**For the following reactions: (A) CH₃CH₂CH₂Br + KOH → CH₃-CH=CH₂ + KBr + H₂O; (B) (CH₃)₃CBr + KOH → (CH₃)₃COH + KBr; (C) Br₂ → Br-Br. Which of the following statement is correct?**

```markdown
(A) Alkyl halide with KOH gives alkene → Elimination reaction (dehydrohalogenation)
(B) Tertiary alkyl halide with KOH gives alcohol → Substitution reaction (SN¹)
(C) Br₂ addition to alkene gives dibromide → Addition reaction
Correct answer is (d).
```
