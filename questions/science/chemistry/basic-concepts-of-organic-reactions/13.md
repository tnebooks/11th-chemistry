**Which of the following represent a series of nucleophiles?**

```markdown
Nucleophiles are electron rich species with lone pairs or negative charge.
CN⁻ (cyanide ion), RCH₂⁻ (carbanion), ROH (alcohol with lone pair on O) all act as nucleophiles.
BF₃, AlCl₃ are electrophiles; H⁺ is electrophile.
Correct answer is (c) CN⁻, RCH₂⁻, ROH.
```
