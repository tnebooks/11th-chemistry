**What is the hybridisation state of benzyl carbonium ion?**

```markdown
Benzyl carbonium ion (C₆H₅-CH₂⁺): The carbon with positive charge is attached to three groups (benzene ring, H, and vacant p orbital) → sp² hybridised, planar geometry.
Correct answer is (a) sp².
```
