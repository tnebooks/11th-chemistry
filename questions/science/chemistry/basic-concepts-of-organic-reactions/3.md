**Decreasing order of nucleophilicity is**

```markdown
Nucleophilicity depends on basicity and polarisability. In polar protic solvents, order: NH₂⁻ > OH⁻ > OCH₃⁻ > RNH₂.
NH₂⁻ is most nucleophilic due to high basicity and less solvation.
Correct answer is (b) NH₂⁻ > OH⁻ > OCH₃ > RNH₂.
```
