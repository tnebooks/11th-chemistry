**Which of the following species does not act as a nucleophile?**

```markdown
BF₃ is electron deficient (boron has only 6 electrons) and acts as an electrophile (accepts electrons), not a nucleophile.
ROH, ROR, and PCl₃ have lone pairs and can act as nucleophiles.
Correct answer is (d) BF₃.
```
