**In a chemical equilibrium, the rate constant for the forward reaction is 2.5 × 10² and the equilibrium constant is 50. The rate constant for the reverse reaction is**

```markdown
Kc = Kf/Kr → Kr = Kf/Kc = 2.5 × 10² / 50 = 250/50 = 5
Correct answer is (b) 5.
```
