---
choices:
  - "The forward reaction is endothermic"
  - "The reaction does not attain equilibrium"
  - "The reverse reaction is exothermic"
answers:
  - "The forward reaction is exothermic"
---

**The equilibrium constant for a reaction at room temperature is K₁ and that at 700 K is K₂. If K₁ > K₂, then**

```markdown
Using van't Hoff equation: ln(K₂/K₁) = -ΔH/R (1/T₂ - 1/T₁)
If T₂ > T₁ and K₁ > K₂, then K₂/K₁ < 1, so ln(K₂/K₁) is negative.
Therefore, ΔH is negative (exothermic). Forward reaction is exothermic.
Correct answer is (a) The forward reaction is exothermic.
```
