**If Kb and Kf for a reversible reaction are 0.8 × 10⁻⁵ and 1.6 × 10⁻⁴ respectively, the value of the equilibrium constant is**

```markdown
For a reversible reaction, equilibrium constant Kc = Kf / Kb
Kc = 1.6 × 10⁻⁴ / 0.8 × 10⁻⁵ = 1.6 × 10⁻⁴ / 8 × 10⁻⁶ = 20
Correct answer is (a) 20.
```
