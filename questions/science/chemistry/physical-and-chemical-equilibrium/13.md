choices:

  - "2x + 0.5"
  - "x + 1"
answers:
  - "x + 0.5"
---

**If x is the fraction of PCl₅ dissociated at equilibrium in the reaction PCl₅ ⇌ PCl₃ + Cl₂, then starting with 0.5 mole of PCl₅, the total number of moles of reactants and products at equilibrium is**

```markdown
PCl₅ initial = 0.5 mol. Let x mol dissociate.
At equilibrium: PCl₅ = 0.5 - x, PCl₃ = x, Cl₂ = x
Total moles = (0.5 - x) + x + x = 0.5 + x
Correct answer is (b) x + 0.5.
```
