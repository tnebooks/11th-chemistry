**Which of the following is not a general characteristic of equilibrium involving physical process?**

```markdown
At equilibrium, the opposing processes do not stop; they continue at the same rate (dynamic equilibrium). The statement "All the physical processes stop at equilibrium" is incorrect. Other statements are correct.
Correct answer is (c).
```
