**[Co(H₂O)₆]²⁺ (aq) (pink) + 4Cl⁻ (aq) ⇌ [CoCl₄]²⁻ (aq) (blue) + 6H₂O (l). In the above reaction at equilibrium, the reaction mixture is blue in colour at room temperature. On cooling this mixture, it becomes pink in colour. On the basis of this information, which one of the following is true?**

```markdown
At room temperature, blue (forward reaction) is favoured. On cooling, pink (reverse reaction) is favoured. Since cooling favors the reverse reaction, the forward reaction must be endothermic (ΔH > 0). Decreasing temperature favors exothermic reaction (reverse), so forward is endothermic.
Correct answer is (a) ΔH > 0 for the forward reaction.
```
