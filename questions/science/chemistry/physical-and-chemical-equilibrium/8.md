**In the equilibrium 2A(g) ⇌ 2B(g) + C₂(g), the equilibrium concentrations of A, B and C₂ at 400 K are 1×10⁻⁴ M, 2.0×10⁻³ M, 1.5×10⁻⁴ M respectively. The value of Kc is**

```markdown
Kc = [B]²[C₂]/[A]² = (2×10⁻³)²(1.5×10⁻⁴)/(1×10⁻⁴)²
= (4×10⁻⁶ × 1.5×10⁻⁴)/(1×10⁻⁸) = 6×10⁻¹⁰/1×10⁻⁸ = 6×10⁻² = 0.06
Correct answer is (a) 0.06.
```
