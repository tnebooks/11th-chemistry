**The values of Kp₁ and Kp₂ for the reactions X ⇌ Y + Z and A ⇌ 2B are in the ratio 9 : 1. If degree of dissociation and initial concentration of X and A be equal then total pressure at equilibrium P₁ and P₂ are in the ratio**

```markdown
For reaction 1: X ⇌ Y + Z (Δn = 1)
For reaction 2: A ⇌ 2B (Δn = 1)
Kp₁ = (α²P₁)/(1-α²)
Kp₂ = (4α²P₂)/(1-α²)
Given Kp₁/Kp₂ = 9/1
(α²P₁)/(4α²P₂) = 9 → P₁/P₂ = 36
Correct answer is (a) 36 : 1.
```
