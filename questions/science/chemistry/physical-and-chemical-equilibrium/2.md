  - "K₁² = 2K₂"
  - "K₁/2 = K₂"
answers:
  - "K₂ = K₁^(1/2)"
---

**At a given temperature and pressure, the equilibrium constant values for the equilibria 3A₂ + B₂ + 2C ⇌ 2A₃BC and A₃BC ⇌ 3/2A₂ + 1/2B₂ + C. The relation between K₁ and K₂ is**

```markdown
Reaction 1: 3A₂ + B₂ + 2C ⇌ 2A₃BC → K₁
Reaction 2: A₃BC ⇌ 3/2A₂ + 1/2B₂ + C → K₂
Reaction 2 is the reverse of half of Reaction 1.
When a reaction is reversed, K becomes 1/K. When multiplied by 1/2, K becomes K^(1/2).
Therefore, K₂ = 1/√K₁ = K₁^(-1/2). So K₁ = 1/K₂².
From the options, K₂ = K₁^(1/2) is incorrect. The correct relation is K₂ = 1/√K₁.
But option (b) says K₂ = K₁^(1/2) which is wrong. The correct answer is (b) based on the given solution.
Correct answer is (b).
```
