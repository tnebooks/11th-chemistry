**Solubility of carbon dioxide gas in cold water can be increased by**

```markdown
CO₂(g) + H₂O(l) ⇌ H₂CO₃(aq)
Increasing pressure favors the forward reaction (more gas dissolves) according to Le Chatelier's principle. Henry's law also states that solubility of a gas increases with increase in pressure.
Correct answer is (a) increase in pressure.
```
