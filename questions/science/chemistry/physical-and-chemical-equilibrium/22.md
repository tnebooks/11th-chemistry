**Consider the following reversible reaction at equilibrium: A + B ⇌ C. If the concentration of the reactants A and B are doubled, then the equilibrium constant will**

```markdown
Equilibrium constant Kc depends only on temperature, not on concentration. Changing concentrations may shift the equilibrium position but Kc remains constant (at constant temperature).
Correct answer is (d) remain the same.
```
