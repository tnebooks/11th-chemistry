**An equilibrium constant of 3.2 × 10⁻⁶ for a reaction means, the equilibrium is**

```markdown
If Kc < 10⁻³, the equilibrium lies largely towards the reactants (reverse direction). Kc = 3.2 × 10⁻⁶ is much less than 1, so products are very small compared to reactants.
Correct answer is (b) largely towards reverse direction.
```
