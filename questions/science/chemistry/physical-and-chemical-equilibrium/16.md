choices:

  - "more Cl₂ will be produced"
  - "none of these"
answers:
  - "more PCl₅ will be produced"
---

**Consider the reaction where Kp = 0.5 at a particular temperature PCl₅(g) ⇌ PCl₃(g) + Cl₂(g). If the three gases are mixed in a container so that the partial pressure of each gas is initially 1 atm, then which one of the following is true?**

```markdown
Q = (P_PCl₃ × P_Cl₂)/P_PCl₅ = (1 × 1)/1 = 1
Kp = 0.5. Since Q > Kp, the reaction will shift to the left (reverse direction) to reach equilibrium. Therefore, more PCl₅ will be produced.
Correct answer is (c) more PCl₅ will be produced.
```
