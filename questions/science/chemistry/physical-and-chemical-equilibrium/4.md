**The formation of ammonia from N₂(g) and H₂(g) is a reversible reaction N₂(g) + 3H₂(g) ⇌ 2NH₃(g) + Heat. What is the effect of increase of temperature on this equilibrium reaction?**

```markdown
The forward reaction is exothermic (Heat is released). According to Le Chatelier's principle, increasing temperature favors the endothermic reaction (reverse reaction). Therefore, equilibrium shifts to the left (towards reactants).
Correct answer is (c) equilibrium is shifted to the left.
```
