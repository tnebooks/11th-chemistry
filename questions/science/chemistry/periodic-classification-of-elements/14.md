**Assertion: Helium has the highest value of ionisation energy among all the elements known. Reason: Helium has the highest value of electron affinity among all the elements known.**

```markdown
Assertion is true - He has the highest IE₁ (2372 kJ/mol). Reason is false - He has the lowest (positive) electron affinity. The high IE is due to its 1s² completely filled stable configuration.
Correct answer is (c) Assertion is true and the reason is false.
```
