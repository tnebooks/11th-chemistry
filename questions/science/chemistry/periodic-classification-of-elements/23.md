**Which of the following pairs of elements exhibit diagonal relationship?**

```markdown
Diagonal relationship exists between elements of the 2nd and 3rd periods: Li-Mg, Be-Al, B-Si. Be (2nd period, Group 2) and Al (3rd period, Group 13) exhibit diagonal relationship.
Correct answer is (d) Be and Al.
```
