**Various successive ionisation enthalpies (in kJ mol⁻¹) of an element are given below: IE₁=577.5, IE₂=1810, IE₃=2750, IE₄=11580, IE₅=14820. The element is**

```markdown
IE₁, IE₂, IE₃ are relatively low. There is a large jump after IE₃ (2750 → 11580). This indicates that after losing 3 electrons, the atom achieves a stable noble gas configuration. Aluminium (Al, Z=13, 1s²2s²2p⁶3s²3p¹) loses 3 electrons to achieve [Ne] configuration. Therefore, the large jump occurs at IE₄.
Correct answer is (c) Aluminium.
```
