**The element with positive electron gain enthalpy is**

```markdown
Argon (Ar) is a noble gas with a completely filled valence shell. It does not accept an electron easily; it requires energy to add an electron, so it has a positive electron gain enthalpy. Hydrogen, Sodium, and Fluorine have negative electron gain enthalpy.
Correct answer is (c) Argon.
```
