**The group of elements in which the differentiating electron enters the anti-penultimate shell of atoms are called**

```markdown
In f-block elements, the differentiating electron (last added electron) enters the (n-2)f orbital, which is the anti-penultimate shell (third last shell). Therefore, f-block elements are the ones where the differentiating electron enters the anti-penultimate shell.
Correct answer is (d) f-block elements.
```
