**How does electron affinity change when we move from left to right in a period in the periodic table?**

```markdown
Electron affinity generally increases from left to right in a period. As atomic size decreases and nuclear charge increases, the tendency to accept an electron increases. (Noble gases are exceptions).
Correct answer is (a) Generally increases.
```
