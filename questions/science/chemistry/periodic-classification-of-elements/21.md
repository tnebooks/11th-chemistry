**Which one of the following is true about metallic character when we move from left to right in a period and top to bottom in a group?**

```markdown
Metallic character: Decreases from left to right in a period (metals to non-metals). Increases from top to bottom in a group (more metallic character). Correct: Decreases in a period and increases along the group.
Correct answer is (a).
```
