**The cause of permanent hardness of water is due to**

```markdown
Permanent hardness of water is caused by soluble chlorides and sulphates of calcium and magnesium, such as CaCl₂, MgCl₂, CaSO₄, MgSO₄. Temporary hardness is caused by bicarbonates like Ca(HCO₃)₂ and Mg(HCO₃)₂.
Correct answer is (c) CaCl₂.
```
