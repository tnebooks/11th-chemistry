**Volume strength of 1.5 N H₂O₂ is**

```markdown
Normality (N) and volume strength relationship: Volume strength = 5.6 × Normality (approximately). More precisely: 1 N H₂O₂ = 5.6 volume. So 1.5 N = 1.5 × 5.6 = 8.4 volume.
Correct answer is (d) 8.4.
```
