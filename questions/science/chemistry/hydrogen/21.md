**Water is a**

```markdown
Water (H₂O) is an amphoteric oxide. It can act as both an acid (donating a proton to form OH⁻) and a base (accepting a proton to form H₃O⁺). In the reaction with HCl, it acts as a base; with NH₃, it acts as an acid.
Correct answer is (c) amphoteric oxide.
```
