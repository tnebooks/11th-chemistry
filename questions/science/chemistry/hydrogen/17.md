**The reaction H₃PO₂ + D₂O → H₂DPO₂ + HDO indicates that hypophosphorus acid is**

```markdown
In H₃PO₂, only one hydrogen atom is attached to oxygen (P-OH), while two hydrogen atoms are directly attached to phosphorus (P-H). Only the hydrogen attached to oxygen is replaceable (acidic). Therefore, hypophosphorus acid is monobasic (one acidic hydrogen). The reaction shows only one H is replaced by D.
Correct answer is (c) monobasic acid.
```
