**Ionic hydrides are formed by**

```markdown
Ionic hydrides (salt-like hydrides) are formed by s-block elements, especially group 1 elements (alkali metals) and group 2 elements. They form hydrides like NaH, CaH₂, etc. Halogens form covalent hydrides, chalcogens form covalent hydrides, and inert gases do not form hydrides.
Correct answer is (d) group one elements.
```
