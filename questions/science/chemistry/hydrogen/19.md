**The type of H-bonding present in ortho nitro phenol and para nitro phenol are respectively**

```markdown
In ortho-nitrophenol, the -OH and -NO₂ groups are close to each other, allowing intramolecular hydrogen bonding. In para-nitrophenol, the -OH and -NO₂ groups are far apart, so they form intermolecular hydrogen bonding with neighboring molecules.
Correct answer is (b) intra molecular H-bonding and inter molecular H-bonding.
```
