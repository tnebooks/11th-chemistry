**Water gas is**

```markdown
Water gas is a mixture of carbon monoxide (CO) and hydrogen (H₂). It is produced by passing steam over hot coke: C + H₂O → CO + H₂. It is used as a fuel and in the production of methanol.
Correct answer is (c) CO + H₂.
```
