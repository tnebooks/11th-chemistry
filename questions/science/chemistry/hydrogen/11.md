**Zeolite used to soften hardness of water is, hydrated**

```markdown
Zeolites used for water softening are hydrated sodium aluminium silicates (Na₂O·Al₂O₃·xSiO₂·yH₂O). They exchange Na⁺ ions with Ca²⁺ and Mg²⁺ ions in hard water. Permutit is a common zeolite used in water softening.
Correct answer is (a) Sodium aluminium silicate.
```
