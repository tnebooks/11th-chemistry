**Assertion: Permanent hardness of water is removed by treatment with washing soda. Reason: Washing soda reacts with soluble calcium and magnesium chlorides and sulphates in hard water to form insoluble carbonates.**

```markdown
Washing soda (Na₂CO₃) removes permanent hardness by precipitating Ca²⁺ and Mg²⁺ as insoluble carbonates: CaCl₂ + Na₂CO₃ → CaCO₃↓ + 2NaCl; MgSO₄ + Na₂CO₃ → MgCO₃↓ + Na₂SO₄. Both assertion and reason are true and reason correctly explains the assertion.
Correct answer is (a).
```
