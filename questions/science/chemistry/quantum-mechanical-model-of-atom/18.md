**How many electrons in an atom with atomic number 105 can have (n+l) = 8?**

```markdown
(n+l) = 8: Possible combinations: (n=4, l=4) → 4g (not possible); (n=5, l=3) → 5f (14 electrons); (n=6, l=2) → 6d (10 electrons); (n=7, l=1) → 7p (6 electrons). For atomic number 105: filled up to 5f (14) + 6d (3) = 17 electrons.
Correct answer is (b) 17.
```
