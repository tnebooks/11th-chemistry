choices:
  - "λ₁/λ₂ = 1"
  - "λ₁ = √(25×50) λ₂"
  - "2λ₁ = λ₂"
answers:
  - "λ₁ = 2λ₂"
---

**The energies E₁ and E₂ of two radiations are 25 eV and 50 eV respectively. The relation between their wavelengths λ₁ and λ₂ will be**

```markdown
E = hc/λ, so E ∝ 1/λ
E₁/E₂ = λ₂/λ₁
25/50 = λ₂/λ₁ → 1/2 = λ₂/λ₁ → λ₁ = 2λ₂
Correct answer is (b) λ₁ = 2λ₂.
```
