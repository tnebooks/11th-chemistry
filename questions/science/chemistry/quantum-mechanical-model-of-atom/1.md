**Electronic configuration of species M²⁺ is 1s² 2s² 2p⁶ 3s² 3p⁶ 3d⁶ and its atomic weight is 56. The number of neutrons in the nucleus of species M is**

```markdown
M²⁺ has 24 electrons (2+2+6+2+6+6 = 24). M atom has 26 electrons (M²⁺ + 2). Therefore atomic number = 26. Number of neutrons = Mass number - Atomic number = 56 - 26 = 30.
Correct answer is (c) 30.
```
