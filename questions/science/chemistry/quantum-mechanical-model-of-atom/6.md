**According to the Bohr Theory, which of the following transitions in the hydrogen atom will give rise to the least energetic photon?**

```markdown
ΔE = E₂ - E₁ = -13.6 (1/n₂² - 1/n₁²) eV
Transitions between higher n values give the least energetic photons. The transition n = 6 to n = 5 has the smallest energy difference. (Transitions to lower n values give higher energy photons).
Correct answer is (d) n = 6 to n = 5.
```
