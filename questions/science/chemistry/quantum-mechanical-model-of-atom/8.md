**Which of the following pairs of d-orbitals will have electron density along the axes? (NEET Phase - II)**

```markdown
dₓ²−y² and d₂² orbitals have electron density along the axes. dₓᵧ, dₓ₂, dᵧ₂ orbitals have electron density between the axes.
Correct answer is (c) d₂², dₓ²−y².
```
