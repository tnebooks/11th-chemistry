**Which one of the following rotates the plane polarized light towards left? (NEET Phase – II)**

```markdown
D(-) Fructose is levorotatory, meaning it rotates plane-polarized light towards the left. D(+) Glucose, L(+) Glucose (though rare), and D(+) Galactose are dextrorotatory. Therefore, the correct answer is D(-) Fructose.
```
