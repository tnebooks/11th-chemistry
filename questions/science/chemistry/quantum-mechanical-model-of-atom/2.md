**The energy of light of wavelength 45 nm is**

```markdown
E = hc/λ
h = 6.626 × 10⁻³⁴ J s, c = 3 × 10⁸ m/s, λ = 45 nm = 45 × 10⁻⁹ m
E = (6.626 × 10⁻³⁴ × 3 × 10⁸) / (45 × 10⁻⁹) = 4.42 × 10⁻¹⁸ J
Correct answer is (c) 4.42 × 10⁻¹⁸ J.
```
