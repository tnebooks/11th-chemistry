choices:

  - "h/4π"
answers:
  - "1/(2m) √(h/π)"
---

**If uncertainty in position and momentum are equal, then minimum uncertainty in velocity is**

```markdown
Heisenberg uncertainty principle: Δx · Δp ≥ h/4π
Δx = Δp = p (assume)
Δp = m Δv
Δx = m Δv
(m Δv)² ≥ h/4π → Δv ≥ 1/(2m) √(h/π)
Correct answer is (c) 1/(2m) √(h/π).
```
