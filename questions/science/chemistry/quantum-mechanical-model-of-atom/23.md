**The energy of an electron in the 3rd orbit of hydrogen atom is -E. The energy of an electron in the first orbit will be**

```markdown
E ∝ -1/n²
E₃ = -E = -13.6/9 eV
E₁ = -13.6 eV = 9 × (-13.6/9) = -9E
Correct answer is (d) -9E.
```
