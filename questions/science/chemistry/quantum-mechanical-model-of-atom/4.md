**Splitting of spectral lines in an electric field is called**

```markdown
Splitting of spectral lines in an electric field is called the Stark effect. Splitting in a magnetic field is called the Zeeman effect. Compton effect is scattering of light. Shielding effect is related to electron screening.
Correct answer is (d) Stark effect.
```
