**At 100°C the vapour pressure of a solution containing 6.5g solute in 100g water is 732mm. If Kb = 0.52, the boiling point of this solution will be**

```markdown
P° = 760 mm, P = 732 mm
ΔP/P° = (760-732)/760 = 28/760 = 0.0368
0.0368 = (6.5/ M₂)/(100/18) = (6.5×18)/(M₂×100)
M₂ = 31.75 g/mol
m = (6.5/31.75) × (1000/100) = 2.047 m
ΔTb = Kb × m = 0.52 × 2.047 = 1.06
Tb = 100 + 1.06 = 101.06 ≈ 101°C
Correct answer is (c) 101°C.
```
