**Which of the following concentration terms is/are independent of temperature?**

```markdown
Molality (a) is independent of temperature because it depends on mass of solvent (which does not change with temperature). Mole fraction (c) is also independent of temperature. Molarity (b) depends on volume, which changes with temperature.
Correct answer is (d) (a) and (c).
```
