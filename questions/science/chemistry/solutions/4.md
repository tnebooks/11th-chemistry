**The partial pressure of nitrogen in air is 0.76 atm and its Henry's law constant is 7.6 × 10⁴ atm at 300K. What is the mole fraction of nitrogen gas in the solution?**

```markdown
Henry's law: P = K_H × x
x = P/K_H = 0.76 / 7.6 × 10⁴ = 1 × 10⁻⁵
Correct answer is (d) 1 × 10⁻⁵.
```
