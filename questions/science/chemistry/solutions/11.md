**The Henry's law constants for two gases A and B are x and y respectively. The ratio of mole fractions of A to B is 0.2. The ratio of mole fraction of B to A dissolved in water will be**

```markdown
P_A = K_H_A × x_A, P_B = K_H_B × x_B
x_B/x_A = (P_B/P_A) × (K_H_A/K_H_B) = (x_B⁰/x_A⁰) × (x/y) = (1/0.2) × (x/y) = 5x/y
Correct answer is (d) 5x/y.
```
