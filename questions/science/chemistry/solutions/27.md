**The freezing point depression constant for water is 1.86°K Kg mol⁻¹. If 5g Na₂SO₄ is dissolved in 45g water, the depression in freezing point is 3.64°C. The Van't Hoff factor for Na₂SO₄ is**

```markdown
ΔTf = i × Kf × m
m = (5/142)/(45/1000) = 0.0352/0.045 = 0.782 m
i = ΔTf/(Kf×m) = 3.64/(1.86×0.782) = 3.64/1.455 = 2.50
Correct answer is (a) 2.50.
```
