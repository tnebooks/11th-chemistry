**Two liquids X and Y on mixing gives a warm solution. The solution is**

```markdown
A warm solution means heat is released (exothermic), so ΔH_mix < 0. This indicates negative deviation from Raoult's law. The solution is non-ideal and shows negative deviation.
Correct answer is (d).
```
