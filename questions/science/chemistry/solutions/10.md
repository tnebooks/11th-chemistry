**Which one of the following binary liquid mixtures exhibits positive deviation from Raoult's law?**

```markdown
Ethanol + water shows positive deviation from Raoult's law because ethanol-water interactions are weaker than ethanol-ethanol and water-water interactions. Acetone + chloroform and HCl + water show negative deviation. Water + nitric acid also shows negative deviation.
Correct answer is (d) ethanol + water.
```
