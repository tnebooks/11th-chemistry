**Which one of the following gases has the lowest value of Henry's law constant?**

```markdown
Henry's law constant is inversely proportional to solubility. The gas with highest solubility has lowest K_H. Among the given gases, CO₂ is the most soluble in water (reacts to form carbonic acid), so it has the lowest Henry's law constant.
Correct answer is (c) CO₂.
```
