**Equimolar aqueous solutions of NaCl and KCl are prepared. If the freezing point of NaCl is -2°C, the freezing point of KCl solution is expected to be**

```markdown
Both NaCl and KCl are 1:1 electrolytes (i=2). For equimolar solutions, they show the same depression in freezing point. So freezing point of KCl = -2°C.
Correct answer is (a) -2°C.
```
