**The KH for oxygen dissolved in water is 4 × 10⁴ atm. If partial pressure of oxygen in air is 0.4 atm, the mole fraction of oxygen in solution is**

```markdown
P = K_H × x → x = P/K_H = 0.4/4×10⁴ = 1 × 10⁻⁵
Correct answer is (c) 1 × 10⁻⁵.
```
