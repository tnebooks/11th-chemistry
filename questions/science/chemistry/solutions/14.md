**At same temperature, which pair of the following solutions are isotonic?**

```markdown
Isotonic solutions have same osmotic pressure. For electrolytes, effective concentration = i × M.
Option (d): Ba(NO₃)₂ (i=3) 0.1×3=0.3; Na₂SO₄ (i=3) 0.1×3=0.3. Both are isotonic.
Correct answer is (d).
```
