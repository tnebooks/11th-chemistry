**What is the molality of a 10% W/W aqueous sodium hydroxide solution?**

```markdown
10% W/W means 10g NaOH in 100g solution → 10g NaOH in 90g water
Moles of NaOH = 10/40 = 0.25 mol
Mass of water = 90g = 0.09 kg
Molality = 0.25/0.09 = 2.78 m
Correct answer is (a) 2.778? Wait 10/90 = 0.111; 0.25/0.09 = 2.78. Option (a) is 2.778. Option (b) is 2.5. The correct is 2.78. So (a) 2.778.
```
