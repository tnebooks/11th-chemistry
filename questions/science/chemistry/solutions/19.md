**The relative lowering of vapour pressure of a sugar solution in water is 3.5 × 10⁻³. The mole fraction of water in that solution is**

```markdown
ΔP/P° = x_solute = 3.5 × 10⁻³
x_solute + x_solvent = 1
x_water = 1 - 3.5 × 10⁻³ = 0.9965
Correct answer is (d) 0.9965.
```
