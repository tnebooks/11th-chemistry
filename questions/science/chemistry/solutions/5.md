**The Henry's law constant for nitrogen gas in water at 350 K is 8 × 10⁴ atm. The mole fraction of nitrogen in air is 0.5. The number of moles of nitrogen dissolved in 10 moles of water at 350K and 4 atm pressure is**

```markdown
Partial pressure of N₂ = 0.5 × 4 = 2 atm
P = K_H × x_N₂ → x_N₂ = 2/8×10⁴ = 2.5 × 10⁻⁵
x_N₂ = n_N₂/(n_N₂ + n_H₂O) = n_N₂/(n_N₂ + 10) ≈ n_N₂/10
n_N₂ = 2.5 × 10⁻⁵ × 10 = 2.5 × 10⁻⁴ mol
Correct answer is (d) 2.5 × 10⁻⁴.
```
