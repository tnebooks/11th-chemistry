  - "α = n(1-i)/(n-1)"
  - "α = n(1-i)/(n(1-i))"
answers:
  - "α = n(i-1)/(1-n)"
---

**The correct equation for the degree of association of an associating solute, 'n' molecules of which undergoes association in solution, is**

```markdown
For association: i = 1 - α + α/n → i = 1 - α(1 - 1/n) → α = n(1-i)/(n-1)
Alternatively, α = (1-i)/(1/n - 1) = n(1-i)/(1-n)
Correct answer is (c) α = n(i-1)/(1-n).
```
