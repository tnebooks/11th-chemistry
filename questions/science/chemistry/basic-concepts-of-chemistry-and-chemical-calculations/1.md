**40 ml of methane is completely burnt using 80 ml of oxygen at room temperature. The volume of gas left after cooling to room temperature is**

```markdown
CH₄ + 2O₂ → CO₂ + 2H₂O
1 ml CH₄ requires 2 ml O₂.
40 ml CH₄ requires exactly 80 ml O₂ (stoichiometric amount).
40 ml CO₂ is produced. H₂O is liquid at room temperature. Therefore, the remaining gas is only 40 ml CO₂.
Correct answer is (a) 40 ml CO₂ gas.
```
