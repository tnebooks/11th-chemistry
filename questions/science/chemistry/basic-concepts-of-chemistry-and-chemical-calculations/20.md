**What is the mass of precipitate formed when 50 ml of 8.5% solution of AgNO₃ is mixed with 100 ml of 1.865% potassium chloride solution?**

```markdown
Mass of AgNO₃ = 50 × 8.5/100 = 4.25g
Mass of KCl = 100 × 1.865/100 = 1.865g
AgNO₃ + KCl → AgCl↓ + KNO₃
Moles AgNO₃ = 4.25/170 = 0.025 mol
Moles KCl = 1.865/74.5 = 0.025 mol
Mass of AgCl = 0.025 × 143.5 = 3.59g
Correct answer is (a) 3.59 g.
```
