**The equivalent mass of a trivalent metal element is 9 g eq⁻¹. The molar mass of its anhydrous oxide is**

```markdown
Equivalent mass = Atomic mass / Valency
Atomic mass = Equivalent mass × Valency = 9 × 3 = 27
Formula of oxide = M₂O₃ (valency 3)
Molar mass = 2 × 27 + 3 × 16 = 54 + 48 = 102 g
Correct answer is (a) 102 g.
```
