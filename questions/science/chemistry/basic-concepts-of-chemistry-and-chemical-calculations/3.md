**Assertion: Two moles of glucose contains 12.044 × 10²³ molecules of glucose. Reason: Total number of entities present in one mole of any substance is equal to 6.02 × 10²²**

```markdown
Assertion is true - 1 mole = 6.022 × 10²³, so 2 moles = 12.044 × 10²³.
Reason is false - Avogadro number is 6.022 × 10²³, not 6.02 × 10²².
Correct answer is (c) assertion is true but reason is false.
```
