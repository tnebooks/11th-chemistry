**The number of water molecules in a drop of water weighing 0.018 g is**

```markdown
Molar mass of H₂O = 18 g/mol
Moles = 0.018/18 = 0.001 mol
Number of molecules = 0.001 × 6.022 × 10²³ = 6.022 × 10²⁰
Correct answer is (c) 6.022 × 10²⁰.
```
