**Carbon forms two oxides, namely carbon monoxide and carbon dioxide. The equivalent mass of which element remains constant?**

```markdown
This is an example of the Law of Multiple Proportions. In CO and CO₂, when the mass of carbon is fixed, the masses of oxygen are in the ratio 1:2. The equivalent mass of oxygen remains constant.
Correct answer is (b) oxygen.
```
