**Two 22.4 litre containers A and B contains 8 g of O₂ and 8 g of SO₂ respectively at 273 K and 1 atm pressure, then**

```markdown
Molar mass O₂ = 32 g/mol, 8g = 0.25 mol
Molar mass SO₂ = 64 g/mol, 8g = 0.125 mol
Molecules ratio = 0.25 : 0.125 = 2 : 1
Container A (O₂) has twice the number of molecules compared to B (SO₂).
Correct answer is (c).
```
