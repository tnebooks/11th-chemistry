**7.5 g of a gas occupies a volume of 5.6 litres at 0°C and 1 atm pressure. The gas is**

```markdown
At STP: 5.6 L = 5.6/22.4 = 0.25 mol
Molar mass = 7.5/0.25 = 30 g/mol
NO has molar mass = 14 + 16 = 30 g/mol
CO = 28, N₂O = 44, CO₂ = 44
Correct answer is (a) NO.
```
