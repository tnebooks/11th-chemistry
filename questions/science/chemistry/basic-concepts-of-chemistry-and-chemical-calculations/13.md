**Which one of the following represents 180g of water?**

```markdown
Molar mass of H₂O = 18 g/mol
180g = 10 moles
Number of molecules = 10 × 6.022 × 10²³ = 6.022 × 10²⁴
Correct answer is (d) 6.022 × 10²⁴ molecules of water.
```
