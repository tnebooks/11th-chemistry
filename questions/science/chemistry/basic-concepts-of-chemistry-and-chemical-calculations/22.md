**Which of the following contain same number of carbon atoms as in 6 g of carbon-12?**

```markdown
6g C-12 = 0.5 mol C = 0.5 × 6.022 × 10²³ = 3.011 × 10²³ atoms
7.5g C₂H₆ (molar mass 30) = 0.25 mol = 0.25 × 6.022 × 10²³ × 2 = 3.011 × 10²³ atoms
8g CH₄ (molar mass 16) = 0.5 mol = 0.5 × 6.022 × 10²³ × 1 = 3.011 × 10²³ atoms
Both have the same number of carbon atoms.
Correct answer is (c) both (a) and (b).
```
