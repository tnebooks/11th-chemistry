**Which of the following compound(s) has/have percentage of carbon same as that in ethylene (C₂H₄)?**

```markdown
C₂H₄: C% = 24/28 × 100 = 85.7%
C₃H₆ (propene): C% = 36/42 × 100 = 85.7%
C₂H₂ (ethyne): C% = 24/26 × 100 = 92.3%
C₆H₆ (benzene): C% = 72/78 × 100 = 92.3%
C₂H₆ (ethane): C% = 24/30 × 100 = 80%
Propene has the same carbon percentage.
Correct answer is (a) propene.
```
