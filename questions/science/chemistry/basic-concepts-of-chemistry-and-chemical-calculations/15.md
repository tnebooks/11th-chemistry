**Total number of electrons present in 1.7 g of ammonia is**

```markdown
Molar mass of NH₃ = 17 g/mol
1.7g = 0.1 mol
1 molecule of NH₃ has 10 electrons (7 from N + 3 from H)
Total electrons = 0.1 × 6.022 × 10²³ × 10 = 6.022 × 10²³
Correct answer is (a) 6.022 × 10²³.
```
