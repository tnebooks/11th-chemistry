**When 22.4 litres of H₂ (g) is mixed with 11.2 litres of Cl₂ (g), each at 273 K and 1 atm, the moles of HCl (g) formed is equal to**

```markdown
H₂ + Cl₂ → 2HCl
At STP: 22.4 L = 1 mol H₂, 11.2 L = 0.5 mol Cl₂.
Cl₂ is the limiting reagent.
0.5 mol Cl₂ produces 1 mol HCl.
Correct answer is (d) 1 mole of HCl (g).
```
