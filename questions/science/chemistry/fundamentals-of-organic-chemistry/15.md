**Which of the following is optically active?**

```markdown
Glucose is optically active (has chiral centers). 3-Chloropentane is optically inactive (meso). 2-Chloropropane is optically inactive. Meso-tartaric acid is optically inactive due to internal compensation.
Correct answer is (d) Glucose.
```
