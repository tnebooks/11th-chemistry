**Ortho and para-nitrophenol can be separated by**

```markdown
Ortho-nitrophenol is steam volatile due to intramolecular hydrogen bonding, while para-nitrophenol is not steam volatile due to intermolecular hydrogen bonding. So they can be separated by steam distillation.
Correct answer is (c) steam distillation.
```
