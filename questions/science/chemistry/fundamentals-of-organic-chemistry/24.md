**An organic compound weighing 0.15g gave on Carius estimation, 0.12g of silver bromide. The percentage of bromine in the compound will be close to**

```markdown
Molar mass of AgBr = 108 + 80 = 188 g/mol
Mass of Br = (80/188) × 0.12 = 0.05106 g
Percentage of Br = (0.05106/0.15) × 100 = 34.04% ≈ 34%
Correct answer is (b) 34%.
```
