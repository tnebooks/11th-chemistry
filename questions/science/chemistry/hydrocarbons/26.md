**An alkane is obtained by decarboxylation of sodium propionate. Same alkane can be prepared by**

```markdown
Decarboxylation of sodium propionate (CH₃CH₂COONa) gives ethane (C₂H₆). Same alkane (ethane) can be prepared by Wurtz reaction of iodomethane (CH₃I + 2Na + CH₃I → C₂H₆).
Correct answer is (b) action of sodium metal on iodomethane.
```
