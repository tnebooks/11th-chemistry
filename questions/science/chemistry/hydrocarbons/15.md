**Cis-2-butene and trans-2-butene are**

```markdown
Cis and trans isomers differ in the spatial arrangement of groups around the double bond. They are configurational isomers (geometrical isomers). They cannot be interconverted without breaking the π bond.
Correct answer is (c) configurational isomers.
```
