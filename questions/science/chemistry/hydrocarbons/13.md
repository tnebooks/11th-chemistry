**(CH₃)₂C=CH₂ on reaction with Cl₂ gives**

```markdown
Addition of Cl₂ to (CH₃)₂C=CH₂ gives the vicinal dichloride. The product is 1,2-dichloro-2-methylpropane (ClCH₂-C(Cl)(CH₃)₂). Actually the major product is 1,2-dichloro-2-methylpropane.
Correct answer is (c) 1,2-dichloro-2-methylpropane.
```
