**The major product formed when 2-bromo-2-methylbutane is refluxed with ethanolic KOH is**

```markdown
Ethanolic KOH causes dehydrohalogenation (elimination) to give the more substituted alkene (Saytzeff rule). 2-bromo-2-methylbutane on elimination gives 2-methylbut-2-ene as the major product (more substituted alkene).
Correct answer is (a) 2-methylbut-2-ene.
```
