**Some meta-directing substituents in aromatic substitution are given. Which one is most deactivating?**

```markdown
Among meta-directing groups, -NO₂ is the most deactivating group due to strong -I and -R effects. It withdraws electrons strongly from the ring.
Correct answer is (b) –NO₂.
```
