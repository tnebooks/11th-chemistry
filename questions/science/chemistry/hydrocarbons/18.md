**Consider the nitration of benzene using mixed conc H₂SO₄ and HNO₃. If a large quantity of KHSO₄ is added to the mixture, the rate of nitration will be**

```markdown
Nitration of benzene involves formation of nitronium ion (NO₂⁺) from HNO₃ in presence of H₂SO₄. KHSO₄ is a weak acid and addition would reduce the concentration of H⁺ ions, thus slowing the formation of NO₂⁺. So the rate of nitration will be slower.
Correct answer is (d) slower.
```
