**In which of the following molecules, all atoms are co-planar?**

```markdown
Molecules with sp² hybridised carbons and no sp³ carbons are planar. Both option (a) and (b) have all atoms in a plane due to sp² hybridisation and conjugated system.
Correct answer is (d) both (a) and (b).
```
