**Peroxide effect (Kharasch effect) can be studied in case of**

```markdown
Peroxide effect (anti-Markovnikov addition) is observed in addition of HBr to unsymmetrical alkenes. It requires an alkene with a terminal double bond. Pent-1-ene (CH₂=CH-CH₂-CH₂-CH₃) is a terminal alkene and shows peroxide effect.
Correct answer is (c) pent-1-ene.
```
