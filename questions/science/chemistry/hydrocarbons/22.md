**Which one of the following is non-aromatic?**

```markdown
A compound is aromatic if it follows Hückel's rule (4n+2 π electrons), is cyclic, planar, and fully conjugated. The compound in option (d) does not satisfy all these criteria and is non-aromatic.
Correct answer is (d).
```
