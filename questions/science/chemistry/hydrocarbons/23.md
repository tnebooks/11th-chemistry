**Which of the following compounds will not undergo Friedel-Crafts reaction easily? (NEET)**

```markdown
Friedel-Crafts reaction requires an electron-rich aromatic ring. Nitrobenzene has a strong electron withdrawing -NO₂ group which deactivates the ring towards electrophilic substitution. It does not undergo Friedel-Crafts reaction easily.
Correct answer is (a) Nitrobenzene.
```
