**C₂H₅Br + 2Na → C₄H₁₀ + 2NaBr. The above reaction is an example of which of the following?**

```markdown
Two alkyl halides react with sodium metal in dry ether to form a higher alkane. This is the Wurtz reaction: 2RBr + 2Na → R-R + 2NaBr.
Correct answer is (b) Wurtz reaction.
```
