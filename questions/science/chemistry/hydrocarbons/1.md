**The correct statement regarding the comparison of staggered and eclipsed conformations of ethane is (NEET)**

In staggered conformation, the hydrogen atoms are as far apart as possible, minimizing electron repulsion and torsional strain. It has no torsional strain and is more stable. Eclipsed conformation has torsional strain due to repulsion between electron clouds of C-H bonds and is less stable.
