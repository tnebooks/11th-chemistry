**Identify the compound 'Z' in the following reaction: C₂H₆O → Al₂O₃, 623K → X → O₃ → Y → Zn/H₂O → Z**

```markdown
C₂H₆O (ethanol) on dehydration with Al₂O₃ at 623K gives ethene (X). Ethene on ozonolysis gives formaldehyde (Z). So Z is formaldehyde.
Correct answer is (a) Formaldehyde.
```
