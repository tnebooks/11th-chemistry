**Which of the following can be used as the halide component for Friedel-Crafts reaction?**

```markdown
Friedel-Crafts reaction requires an alkyl halide (not aryl or vinyl halide) because aryl and vinyl halides do not form carbocations easily. Isopropyl chloride is an alkyl halide and can be used.
Correct answer is (d) isopropyl chloride.
```
