**The C-H bond and C-C bond in ethane are formed by which of the following types of overlap?**

```markdown
In ethane, carbon is sp³ hybridised. C-H bond is formed by overlap of sp³ orbital of carbon with s orbital of hydrogen (sp³-s). C-C bond is formed by overlap of two sp³ orbitals (sp³-sp³).
Correct answer is (a).
```
