**Which among the following alkenes on reductive ozonolysis produces only propanone?**

```markdown
2,3-dimethylbut-2-ene (CH₃)₂C=C(CH₃)₂ on ozonolysis gives two molecules of propanone (acetone). Ozonolysis cleaves the double bond and each carbon becomes a carbonyl group.
Correct answer is (d) 2,3-Dimethyl but-2-ene.
```
